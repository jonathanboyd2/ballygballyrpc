OUR MOTTO

What it means

“Passion for Jesus, Compassion for people”

Our motto “Passion for Jesus, Compassion for people” is based on the great commandment that Jesus speaks of in Matthew 22:37-40. Jesus explains that the whole of the law boils down to “love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your mind.” And “love your neighbor as yourself.”

As Christian churches in Ballygawley & Ballyreagh, we believe that Jesus is the second person of the Trinity and the divine Son of God, so is worthy of our worship. We express our worship for God in our passionate pursuit of Jesus as our Saviour and Lord (John 14:23).

Out of our love for Jesus and as a response to God’s love for us, we seek to lovingly care for one another and has compassion on others.
